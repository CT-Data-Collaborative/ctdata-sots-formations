Exploring Business Formation Patterns in Connecticut

In partnership with the Connecticut Secretary of the State, The Connecticut Data Collaborative is excited to present business formation data from {{ startMonth }} {{ startYear }} to {{ endMonth }} {{ endYear }}. This is the first time this data has been made open and accessible.

Here you will find three ways to explore the data: Visualize formations over time, compare a subset of towns, or view monthly starts and stops. You can also search for businesses on our dedicated search portal.

Formations over time

Explore formation patterns by county or town by year, quarter or month, and by business type. Visualize the data on a map or in a bar chart.

Compare Towns

Interested in a particular town's data? Or have a group of towns you want to explore? This tool allows you to compare data for a particular town against 4 additional towns of your choosing.

Starts & Stops

General overview of the number of business entities forming/registering and dissolving/withdrawing in a given month.
